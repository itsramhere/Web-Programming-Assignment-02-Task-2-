Get To Know Me

Hi! I am V V Ramarathinam Iyer, Ram for short! I'm really glad you took the time out of your day to visit my website- I hope you find what you're looking for when you go through my portfolio! I'm a 2nd year B.Tech CSE student at VIT University, Vellore.

I am a currently in-progress backend developer, exploring and learning concepts on the go daily, and building my knowledge and skill-set! Additionally, I do solve problems on LeetCode and CodeForces as well. Feel free to contact me for queries of any sort that you might have.
You can usually find me at the library studying or in my room sleeping, you decide where you want to catch up! But be warned, I am someone who loves his sleep :D

That's all here - do roam around and explore the site for anything you find interesting!

My Daily Life

I get up early, usually 2 hours before class time- go for a jog which I just go to pet cats and come back and get ready (and wake up my roommates while I'm at it.)
This is usually followed by the usually shindig of attending classes and labs. If I have some time in between classes I either sleep or finish my remaining work.
When the day's classes are done - I come back to my bed which looks at me with loving eyes and just crash on it. Well, that's the best way I could possibly describe how my days go.

  1. Morning Jog

  2. College classes

  3. Evening Relaxation and walks with friends

  4. Coding Practice
